Detailed Description

The time signature structure. The time signature defines the metrics of a "measure" or bar of music. For examplke 4/4 means the measure is defined by 1/4 notes and that 4 of them define a measure or bar. Another example 6/8 means the measure is divided by 1/8 notes and there are 6 of them in a measure. We are experimenting with fractional time signatures this means that we can have weird signatures like 2.5/4.5 which means the measure is made of 1/4.5 notes and there are 2.5 of them in a measure
