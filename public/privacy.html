Privacy Policy

Please note, this is currently a closed ‘alpha’ version of the StateSong app. ‘Alpha’ applications are in a ‘developer test’ mode and, generally, not released to the public until in a ‘Beta’ status. There is no charge nor limits on the use of this version.

Also, while no personal data is, or ever will be retained by MediMuse, we do collect unhandled or unexpected application errors through the Microsoft App Center and through Google Play Store. This allows us to update the app as we develop a Beta version, which will show both functional & interface improvements. We ask for recording permission but we do not record from the microphone on the users device.  The recording is for the user to capture music generated by the application.
